1 //Portable Assembly (PASM)
2 /*
3  * Portable assembly is a new low-level IR designed to present a close correlation
4  * between the software and architecture. It is a typed language and is designed
5  * to be more easily translated into Assembly than LTAC.
6  *
7  * This header contains the types for PASM. I haven't decided yet what I'm going to
8  * do with LTAC, it will depend on how this works out.
9  *
10  * For now, I will be creating a text version of PASM to test the translation into
11  * Assembly before porting it to the other parts of the compiler
12 */
